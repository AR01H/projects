Our Story

Born from Frustration, Built with Purpose

"We spent 14 months trying to buy our first home. Every estate agent we spoke to was working for the seller — not us. We knew there had to be a better way."

After experiencing the home-buying process firsthand — and witnessing hundreds of buyers overpay, miss red flags, and make decisions without proper information — our founder set out to build a truly buyer-centric service.

Today, Advaith Homes has helped over 500 buyers across the UK save an average of £18,000 each — while reducing the stress, confusion, and wasted time that defines the traditional property purchase experience.
