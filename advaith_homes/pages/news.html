POLICY 28 Apr 2026

Stamp Duty Changes: First-Time Buyer Thresholds Updated — Act Before the Deadline

The temporary stamp duty relief for first-time buyers reverted in April 2025. Here's the current picture and what buyers can do to minimise tax liability.

Since April 2025, the temporary first-time buyer stamp duty threshold dropped from £425,000 back to £300,000, meaning many first-time buyers in England now face additional tax costs.

Current stamp duty rates for first-time buyers:

  • Up to £300,000: 0%
  • £300,001 – £500,000: 5%
  • Above £500,000: Standard rates apply (no FTB relief)
