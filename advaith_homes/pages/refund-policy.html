Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2024

We want every client to feel confident engaging Advaith Homes. This policy sets out when and how fees are refunded.

1. Free Consultation

Our initial consultation is always free of charge. No payment is taken at consultation stage and no refund is necessary.

2. Research Reports

Property Research Reports and Legal Search Pack reviews are provided as standalone one-off services. Once a report has been delivered, fees are non-refundable as the service has been fully rendered. If we are unable to complete a report, a full refund will be issued.

3. Full Service & Negotiation Packages

For ongoing service packages, the following applies:

  • Cancellation within 14 days of signing (cooling off period): Full refund of any upfront fee paid, provided no substantive work has commenced.
  • Cancellation after 14 days where no property has been found: Refund of any upfront retainer minus reasonable costs incurred (administration, searches conducted, time spent).
  • Cancellation after offer accepted: No refund of fees earned at this stage, as substantial service has been rendered.
  • Property purchase falls through (not client's fault): At our discretion, we will typically waive any further fees and assist in finding an alternative property.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at refunds@advaithhomes.co.uk with your name, engagement date, and reason for the request. We will respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 10 business days to the original payment method.

5. Disputes

If you are unhappy with a refund decision, you may escalate to our Director. We are committed to treating all clients fairly and resolving disputes promptly and reasonably.
