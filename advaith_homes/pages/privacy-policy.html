Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Advaith Homes Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in accordance with the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Advaith Homes Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales. We operate as a buyer's agent, providing property acquisition services to home buyers across the UK. Our registered address is available upon request.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity data: name, username, date of birth
  • Contact data: address, email, telephone numbers
  • Financial data: budget information (but never bank details or card numbers)
  • Property preference data: requirements, wish lists, search criteria
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, from website visits
  • Communications: messages, emails, call notes from our interactions

3. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data to: provide our buyer's agent services; communicate with you about your property search; send relevant property information and market updates (with your consent); improve our services; and comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data on the basis of: performance of a contract (to provide our services); legitimate interests (to run and improve our business); consent (for marketing communications); and legal obligation (where required by law).

5. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. We may share data with: our professional service providers (solicitors, surveyors) where necessary for your purchase; IT service providers who process data on our behalf; and regulatory authorities where legally required.

6. Data Retention

We retain client data for 7 years after completion of services, in accordance with legal and regulatory requirements. Marketing preferences data is held until you unsubscribe.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to: access your personal data; correct inaccurate data; request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations); object to processing; and data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@advaithhomes.co.uk.

8. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality and analytics cookies (with consent) to improve the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Contact Us

For privacy matters: privacy@advaithhomes.co.uk or write to our registered address. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
