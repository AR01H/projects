About Stamp Duty Land Tax

Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) is a tax you pay when buying a property or land over a certain price in England and Northern Ireland.

The amount you pay depends on the purchase price, whether you're a first-time buyer, and whether the property is your main residence or an additional property.
